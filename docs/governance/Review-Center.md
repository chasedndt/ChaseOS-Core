# Review Center

The Review Center evaluates proposed outputs before they become durable truth, shipped artifacts, or public releases.

## Review dimensions

- correctness;
- source grounding;
- privacy/sanitization;
- authority compliance;
- test/verification evidence;
- user-facing clarity.

## Verdicts

- `pass` — ready for the stated next step.
- `conditional_pass` — acceptable after named fixes.
- `blocked` — cannot proceed until blockers are resolved.
