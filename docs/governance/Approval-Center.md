# Approval Center

The Approval Center is the review surface for proposed actions that need operator or policy approval.

## What belongs here

- export updates;
- write-capable workflow runs;
- publication actions;
- connector activation;
- credential-bound operations;
- canonical promotion requests.

## Approval record

An approval record should include scope, requester, action, target, allowed writes, expiration, and verification requirements.

Approval is specific. It does not grant broad future authority.
